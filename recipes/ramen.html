ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 dash black pepper
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced diagonally
  • 1 stalk celery, sliced diagonally
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally

steps:

  1. Heat the broth, soy sauce, ginger, black pepper, carrot, celery, red pepper, green onions and garlic in a 2-quart saucepan over medium-high heat to a boil.
  2. Stir the noodles and chicken in the saucepan.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium and cook for 10 minutes or until the noodles are done.
